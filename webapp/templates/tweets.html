About Page

In this never resting world keeping ourselves updated is not less than an arduous task. People are so busy with their schedules that they don't know what's happening around them. But fortunately, Twitter makes it easy for those busy bees. Twitter keeps one up to date by sharing the latest news about various fields that the respective person is interested in. But wait for a second! How is it different or better than the news channels? Well, Twitter only provides you with the news you are interested in. It avoids unnecessary feeds which may consume your time. Most of the websites too have a Twitter section so that the user may get the latest trends or news related to the respective content. For example in an online medical website, may keep the tweets of the ministry of the health department, so that the user comes to know about the latest guidelines set by the authorities and may take the necessary preventives. That's why twitter must be included .
